** Pizza **

Ingredients:

pizza dough
pizza sauce (see sauce recipe)
green peppers
mushrooms
pineapple
mozarella cheese

Instructions:

1. Spread dough evenly in pan.  Toss first if you like.
2. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough.
3. Sprinkle an even layer of cheese over the sauce.
4. Dice green peppers; sprinkle on top of cheese.
5. Add mushrooms to pizza.
